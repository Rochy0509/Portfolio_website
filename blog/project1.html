GRASP01 Humanoid Robotic Arm

A fully 3D-printed humanoid robotic arm with 6 degrees of freedom (DOF), designed and programmed from scratch in my Computer-Aided Design class. It mimics human arm motion for precise, versatile tasks. I am now enhancing it with ROS2 and robust compliant controls like impedance and gravity compensation for improved adaptability and safety.

Additional details: This project also addressed challenges like weight distribution and precise motion control, using advanced 3D printing techniques and innovative materials to create a functional prototype.
